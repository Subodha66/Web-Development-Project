Introducing Leon's, Our New Restaurant (Opening Early Fall 2024)

We’re thrilled to share that we’re hard at work bringing an exciting new concept to life. Named after Natalie’s great grandfather, Leon’s will be an all-day neighborhood Italian restaurant with Egyptian and French accents that reflects her family’s roots, our shared love of Italian food & culture, and inspired interpretations of treasured family culinary traditions and recipes. Our vision is for a convivial gathering place where guests from all over will join us from early morning until late in the night, serving continuously from breakfast through dinner.

We’re looking forward to updating you on all the fun in the months to come!
